Submission Instructions


Submitted papers must contain at most 6 pages (8.5" x 11" (letter) two-column format, using 10-point type on 11-point leading, with a maximum text block of 7" wide x 9" deep with an intercolumn spacing of .25"). Submissions with a page size of 11.7 x 8.3 " (297 x 210 mm / A4) will not be accepted. The page limits include figures, tables, and references. Authors may use the ACM templates for convenience (e.g., on Overleaf here). All submissions must be written in English and should contain the authors' names, affiliations, and contact information.

The instructions for the camera-ready paper can be found on EWSN's website here: EWSN Camera-ready Instructions.

Accepted papers will be included in the conference proceedings. The proceedings will be indexed in the ACM Digital Library, SCOPUS, and other prominent digital libraries. Authors of accepted papers are expected to present their work in a plenary session as part of the main workshop program.

Page limits: max 6 pages. These limits include references, tables, and any appendix.

Papers must be submitted electronically through HotCRP.

The deadline for submitting the camera-ready is July 18.

Important Dates
Paper submission June 01, 2025 (AoE, 23:59 UTC-12)
Notification of acceptance: July 15, 2025
Camera-ready: July 18, 2025
Workshop date: September 22, 2025
